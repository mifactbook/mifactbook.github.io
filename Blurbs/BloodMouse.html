--- layout: creatures title: Blood Mouse mob_id: 304 ---

The Blood Mouse is a rodent with a taste for flesh. It gets its name from the blood red patches of fur that line its cheeks. It makes it look like it's just eaten a very bloody dinner. If you get in its way, it may try to make a meal out of you, too. Bash, slash, or smash it to a bloody pulp if you want to battle it.
