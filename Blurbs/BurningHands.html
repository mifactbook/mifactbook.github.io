Conjurer spell

This entertaining combat spell causes jets of intense flame to spring from the finger tips of the caster. These flames can extend in an arc out to about 30 hands, literally frying everything in the area. To cast this Spell in combat, issue the Cast Spell order: C 917. It uses 20 Spell Points each time it is cast.
