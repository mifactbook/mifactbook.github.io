A Kongo Bow has only one purpose: To kill a Dragon. When combined with a Black Arrow, it can, with luck and courage, strike to the heart of even a Dragon and slay it. To make a Kongo Bow, you must chop a branch from the uppermost reaches of a Kongo Mongo Tree and fashion it into a slender and powerful bow. It takes 10 bowstrings to get a proper pull. To make a Kongo Bow, issue the M 151 order in a Kongo Mongo Tree square. It takes 150 APs and 10 Bow Strings. You can carry 1.
