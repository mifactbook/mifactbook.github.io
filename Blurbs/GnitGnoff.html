Once a swarm of Gnitgnats latch on to you they can be a real pain. Every week one of their number breaks off and attacks you. Now, there is something you can do about this unpleasant situation. Just make your- self a batch of Gnitgnoff (pronounced ga-nit-ga-noff). This Voodoo concoction can be obtained by mixing 1 Guano Plasm, 1 Purple Lotus Leaf, and 1 Oil into a Cauldron. Stir for 4 Action Points. To make, write the MAKE order: M 98. To consume, write the USE/CONSUME order: U 98. Consuming Gnitgnoff changes your scent, causing the Gnitgnat swarm following you to leave the area. You can carry 7 batches.
