This large tree-dweller likes to hang from branches in Sand & Palms terrain and then drop upon unsuspecting foes. Be wary of its sharp, three-toed arms. You'll note that it is brown with white streaking while furiously wrestling with it. Its floppy eye pouches give the Gargantu Tree- Sloth a sad appearance, disguising its true mean streak.
