--- layout: items item_name: Damage Silver Knife item_id: 311 ---

This knife is partially broken. In its current state it's not equipable and of no use. However, it can be repaired by a Jossman Forger for a reasonable cost. A Silver Knife is a Damage Class 1 Pointed Weapon and you may carry a pair of them. Because this knife is crafted with silver, it is particularly effective against foes who are vulnerable to silver weaponry. You can carry two.
