--- layout: creatures title: McMacaw mob_id: 154 ---

Bright and multi-colored, this bird resembles a very large parrot...with a stuttering problem. Unfortunately its pleasing appearance hardly compensates for its loud, annoying gibb-gibb-erish. Squaawk! To correct this, a well-timed whack with a Driftclub will quiet it permanently. The McMacaw is found somewhere near the coast because its favorite food is sligg turtle eggs.
