--- layout: creatures title: Terapus mob_id: 151 ---

A denizen of hilly terrain is the living menace, a Terapus. Standing 20-24 hands tall, this dinosaur-like Creature sprouts two heads from its short neck. Its plump body rides atop 14 legs. If this Creature didn't have a brain the size of a pea, it would rule the hills. Still, it comes close to doing so. This is definitely one of those Creatures that all but the very experienced Monsters should run from.
