Followers of Yasmin

This offensive battle Spell is considered Yasmin El Boa's greatest gift to her followers. The Venom Strike spell allows Yasmin's followers to spew forth a glowing ball of viscous venom. This attack has been known to literally eat the flesh from the bones of its victims. Those fortunate enough to survive it may carry the remnants of its poison in their system for weeks, slowly killing them (in excruciating pain) long after they have fled the field of battle. To make use of this gift, issue the CAST SPELL ORDER: C 966. It takes 16 Action Points each time it is cast.
