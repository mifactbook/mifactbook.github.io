Equipping

Most of the items carried by your Monster are stowed in your Haversuit. But some items you will need ready in case of combat. There are over a dozen categories of items with which you can equip yourself (e.g. Defensive Weapon). Whenever you obtain an item for a category in which you are not currently equipped you will automatically equip yourself with it. If you wish to equip yourself with a different item use the EQUIP order: E (Item#). If you want to unequip an item and not replace it with another, write an EQUIP order and specify the Item# you wish removed. When an equipped item is dropped, xfered or sold you will automatically unequip it first.
