Long-dead warriors for Vlados Xi, Ghasts retain an inkling of what it was like to be living. This causes them to be much more cunning fighters than the more common Ghouls. They also have a more burning hatred of the living as they envy them for all they have lost. Avoid the spindly arms and legs unless you want the voodoo enhanced nails to rip your flesh from the bone. And stay away from its bone-cracking teeth unless you want to become the main course at the local Banquet of the Undead.
