This is one dangerous avian. Picture a large buzzard with the beak, talons and speed of an eagle. But it's bigger than an eagle and buzzard combined with an unsavory disposition. The Hawk Buzzard has a predilection for Large Caves; it's about the only place you'll find them. Not that you'd ever be looking for one.
