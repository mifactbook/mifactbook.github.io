Are you tired of taking unnecessary blows in combat? Would you like to take less damage? Then make yourself a Crab Buckler! This small, brown shield can be carried as a defensive weapon of Armor Class 1. To make it, issue the MAKE order: M 102. It takes 14 Action Points and you need 8 Crab Exoscales. You can carry just one.
