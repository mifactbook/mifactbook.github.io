The idea more than once crosses your mind that a Carved Club doesn't do sufficient damage, but that if you enhanced it, it would. Porququills would do the trick! If you acquire fifteen quills from the Porqubeast you can drive them into your Carved Club and create a Spiked Club. Then not only can you bash the Creatures that attack but you can punch a few holes in them to boot. To fashion a Spiked Club you need a Carved Club, 15 Porququills, and 45 Action Points. Issue the MAKE order: M 45. The Spiked Club is a Bashing weapon of Damage Class 4. You can carry one. [Note: If you're currently equipped with a Carved Club and make a Spiked Club, you'll automatically be equipped with the Spiked Club.]
