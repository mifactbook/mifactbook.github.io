Silver Knuckles stand as one of the great achievements of Zoingot Science. You may be a poor to average wrestler, but With them, you can be an awesome wrestling demon capable of toasting even a Blood Mummy with a single punch. Of course they take up room in your inventory. Room that you could use for something else if you could. Well, now you can. Stone Knuckles is a situational spell that you will use whenever you get in a wrestling match. It only costs you 3 Spell Point to use and is just as effective as wearing five sliver knuckles.
