This is a counter spell to both Spell# 41 [Detect Fellow Followers] and 42 [Detect Rival Followers]. It hides you and any fellow Followers in your square from magical searches. Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 43. It takes 6 Action Points and uses 6 Spell Points.

Note: This spell is not reliable at hiding Monsters inside Forts, Far Inns, etc. This spell is strongest at preventing detection on the day cast. The spell's power gradually weakens and will totally dissipated after 24 days.
