--- layout: items item_name: Grumble Amulet item_id: 112 ---

Made from Grumbel feathers, this magical necklace is worn by elite Furrcats. True, it is one of the most beautiful Amulets, and this may be why Furrcats are percieved to be the coolest of the Seven Monster Races. The Amulet's power manifests whilst a Furrcat is hunting; success is more frequent. Naturally, only a Furrcat knows how to use this properly - other races cannot even equip it. You can carry 2.
