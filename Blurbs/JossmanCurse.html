Jossman Curse

So you've ignored the Lore Master, disturbed a Stone Crypt, killed a Skeletal Jossman and are probably now wondering what has happened. Wherever you go Jossmen will be able to sense your foul presence. You will be unable to interact with any Far structures. Furthermore fellow monsters will feel more hostile towards you. If I were in your boots, I'd try and find a way to get rid of this curse pronto, before someone makes a ghost of you.
