Bignose Ancestral Ceremony

To perform the Ceremony, you must first gather Stinkfruit. To do this, issue the GATHER STINKFRUIT order: GX. (This takes no Action Points and is a Standing Order.) The more stinkfruit gathered and the more Bignoses present, the more honor given to your Ancestors. Contact the three others of your race with whom you washed ashore and make arrangements to perform the Ceremony. Someone needs to choose a square where all of you will meet. After everyone has arrived, someone should issue the ANCESTRAL CEREMONY order: AC. It takes 12 Action Points. It's best done from 32-40 days of arriving on the island. [After your sixth turn is processed, it's too late to perform the Ceremony]. As few as one Monster can perform the Ceremony and more than four can, too.
