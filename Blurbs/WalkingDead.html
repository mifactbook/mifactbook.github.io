A talented Magician can take a sun-bleached pile of bones and, with the proper incantation, can turn it into a killing fury capable of causing the demise of unsuspecting travellers. A cautious Ruk should have little trouble disassembling this pile of bones but more than a couple can get to be exhausting, given that they seldom stay disassembled for long. They also come equipped with a Black Iron Helm, a memory of their former life. If you can take one down, you can collect this as booty.
