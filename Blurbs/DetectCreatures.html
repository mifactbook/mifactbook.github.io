This spell allows you to detect the presence of Creatures within two squares of you in all directions (covers a 5x5 area of squares). You'll learn which species occupy each square. Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 77. It takes 24 Action Points and uses 12 Spell Points.

Note: Mounts being ridden and captured creatures are not detected with this spell.
