--- layout: blurbs title: Knolltir blurb: |

When you think of puny Monsters you think of the Knolltir. When you think of weasly, scummy, and not terribly bright Monsters, you think of the Knolltir. Picture a thin Orcish type, equipped with a Jagged Bodkin, spear or club. And sometimes a Sling. All but the most inexperienced of the Seven Monster Races simply grind Knolltors into hamburgers. However, there's a saying that where there's one Knolltir there's a dozen more. And that can be a problem. Battle is very tiring to anyone...and once fatigue sets in, the next Knolltir may get lucky. They live in bands, usually in hilly areas. They wear Leather Armor and often sport a Squzskin Helm. Their voices are high-pitched but not very loud.
