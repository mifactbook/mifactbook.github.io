Whether you've drank from a scorpoisoned waterhole or been bitten by a poisonous creature, this is the spell for you. Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 17. It uses 20 spell points and 10 action points, and provides instant relief from almost all poisons. Sadly, it cannot be cast quickly enough to counteract the effects of Shingle Poison.
