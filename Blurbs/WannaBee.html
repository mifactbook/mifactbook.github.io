This flying insect doesn't buzz like its smaller cousin the Bludgeon Bee, but makes an irritating whining noise. It is always on the move, probably on the lookout for the best site to build its hive. Luckily few ever find a site worthy of them, so they are a rare species. It attacks with its dart-like stinger, and is fairly hard to hit well because it moves around so quickly. If you do hit one with a Bashing weapon it makes a very satisfying "Splat!" sound.
