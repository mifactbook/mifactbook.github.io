--- layout: items item_name: Damaged Red Iron Helm item_id: 409 ---

Glowing with the red of blood, this Damaged Red Iron Helm once sat upon the head of someone obsessed with the glory of battle. Apparently, someone else got the best of them and they lost their helm (or worse). With a little help from a Forger this helm will provide excellent protection. Until then, you'll have to settle for Armor Class 2 protection. You can carry two.
