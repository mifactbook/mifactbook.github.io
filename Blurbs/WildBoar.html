--- layout: creatures title: Wild Boar mob_id: 108 ---

Catch one of these and you'll have the tastiest meal on this side of the Island. Lotsa leftovers too. One thing though: They're real tough to kill without a Spear and they can knock the crap out of you in a charge. The Wild Boar comes in several skin tones, ranging from tan to dark grey. It's known to roam many types of terrain.
