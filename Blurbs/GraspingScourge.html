Tired of that lame Dactyl Whip that just doesn't cut it with the tougher foes? Here is the answer for you. The Grasping Scourge. Made from 8 Tentaculus Tentacles and 4 Squzskins, this nasty weapon does Level 5 Whip damage. If you find yourself in possession of the required items, issue the MAKE Order: M 214. You will set about using the squzskins to fashion a nice, comfortable handle to which you will firmly attach the 8 tentacles. When used, the suckers on the tentacles will grasp at the skin or armour of your foes and, if you are skillful, pull off a nice-sized piece or two. You can carry only one.
