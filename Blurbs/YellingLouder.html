Yelling Louder

You are told the secret to Yelling louder and louder over time is to Yell once every turn. This will build up the strength of your vocal cords. Should you become "Yelled Out" as a result of trying to Yell two or more times in a turn, you'll need to acquire a Yellow Lotus Elixir to cure this malady.
