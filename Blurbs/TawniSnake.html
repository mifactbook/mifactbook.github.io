--- layout: creatures title: Tawni Snake mob_id: 177 loc: - name: "Koma Den" url: "../Locations/KomaDen.html" ---

This orange and black Jungle snake loves to cling to the bodies of larger Creatures and Monsters. About 6 hands long, it's not so dangerous in battle because its bite isn't severe or poisonous. However, it can take a lot of damage and is one of the scarier-looking varieties of snake. A Whip Weapon is best against it.
