Because it is both a Primary Weapon and great for hunting, the Crude Spear is one of the most useful weapons around. To make a Crude Spear, issue the MAKE order: M 3. You need to be in Sticks terrain and spend 30 Action Points searching for a bamboo pole of the correct length, balance and sharpness. A Crude Spear is a Pole weapon of Damage Class 2. Only one may be carried.
