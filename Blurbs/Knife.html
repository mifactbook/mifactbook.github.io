In Low Hills terrain, there are often pieces of flint that can be honed to make a fine Knife. A Knife is probably the most useful item you'll ever get your hands on as you'll need one to make many other kinds of items. To make a Knife, issue the MAKE order: M 7. This takes 12 Action Points. A Knife is a Damage Class 1 Pointed Weapon and you may carry a pair of them.
