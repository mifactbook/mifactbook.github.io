--- layout: items item_name: PigIron Mail item_id: 185 ---

Now this is some serious armour. Made from individual rings of PigIron and underlain by a supple leather, this armour is extremely large and extremely heavy. Since it is made for Trolls, it will seldom be small enough to fit a Ruk. If you are lucky enough to get one it takes no less than 850 muscle to carry its enormous weight. If you are lucky enough to come into posession of a suit of this armour, keep in mind you can only carry one. It provides Body Armour Level 5 protection.
