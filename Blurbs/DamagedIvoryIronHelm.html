--- layout: items item_name: Damaged Ivory Iron Helm item_id: 407 ---

What more could a Knight in Shining Armor want than a shiney Ivory Iron Helm? This Damaged Ivory Iron Helm is the next best thing. It's obviously been used and abused and is in need of the attention of a good Forger, but it still provides Armor Class 2 protection and can be readily mended. If you are lucky enough to find as many, you can carry up to two.
