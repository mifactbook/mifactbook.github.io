Killing a Jungle Rattler gets you two Rattler Darts. You have to (very carefully) remove its fangs to get the darts. They are excellent projectiles when shot from a Blow Pipe - Damage Class 5. However, because they are not perfectly straight, it takes more skill to hit with one than with a Zinki Dart (for example). You may carry 6 of these darts.
