At times it would be nice to have a Bashing weapon to use against a particular Creature. If you search Low Hills terrain it is possible to find a fallen hardwood tree. Use your Knife and a branch from such a tree to fashion yourself a Carved Club. To do this use the MAKE order: M 4. This takes 24 Action Points. A Carved Club is Damage Class 2 and you can carry just one.
