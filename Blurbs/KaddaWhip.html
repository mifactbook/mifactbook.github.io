The Kadda vine is one of the rarer vines in High Jungle terrain, the land of vines. If you lack a Kaddawhip and come upon a Kadda vine, you'll acquire one, the most basic of whips. It is a Damage Class 2 weapon. Only the lesser Boddens wield it in battle. You can carry one.
