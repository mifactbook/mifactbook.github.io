Need an extract edge in battle? This defensive battle spell increases your chances of hitting and avoiding been hit during missile fire and the melee parts of battle. Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 811. It uses up 15 Spell Points each time it's cast. You'll only use this spell against the tougher of opponents.
