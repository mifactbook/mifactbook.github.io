What?!? Carbunkle?!? Now you are in real trouble. Hope you said your prayers. But wait just a minute. That's not Carbunkle. That is A Carbuncle, a smallish creature of Swampland that is notorious for its extremely hard shell and nasty disposition. It may not be as tought as the original, but it will give you a very healthy fight.
