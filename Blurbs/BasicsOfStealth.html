Basics of Stealth

Your Jossman instructor teaches you all sorts of things. This includes how to move quietly by walking in a different way, how to hide behind a tree properly, do's and don'ts, such as not making sudden movements, and the theory of zigzaging your movement within a square. Most of all, you're instilled with the importance of always trying to take advantage of any available cover. Your Stealth rises significantly as a result of this instruction.
