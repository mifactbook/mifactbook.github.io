This is the perfecti bone removed from a dead dragon. Long ago, this radiant white bone had several exotic uses. One was by a Magesmith as a primary component for making a Dragon staff. Another involved a rite conducted by Followers of Kadar, the Dragon demigod. This item can also be placed in any god's Temple Treasure room. You can carry 2.
