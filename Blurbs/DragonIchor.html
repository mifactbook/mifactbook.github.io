--- layout: items item_name: Dragon Ichor item_id: 380 ---

Unbeknownst to many, Dragons posess magical blood. When a dragon dies a strange thing happens during rigor mortis. In several places throughout a Dragon's body, its blood coagulates and then solidifies, forming a chunky substance known as Dragon Ichor. Ancient wizards found Dragon Ichor to be a very useful and valuable substance in their experiments. Dragon Ichor can be placed in a god's Temple Treasure Room. You can carry 7 chunks of it.
