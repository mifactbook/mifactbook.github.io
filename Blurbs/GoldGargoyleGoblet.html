--- layout: items item_name: Gold Gargoyle Goblet item_id: 367 ---

This interesting-looking, gargoyle-shaped goblet is an ancient relic vessel. It is believed to have been made by Graggolos, the Gargoyle Demigod. It's said that when certain potions and elixirs are drunk from it, special powers are gained. In particular, powers relating to spells. This item is also a welcome addition to any god's Temple Treasure Room. You can carry 3.
