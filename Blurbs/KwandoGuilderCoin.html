--- layout: items item_name: Kwando Guilder Coin item_id: 366 ---

This is the common coin of the Kwandonian Kaligarchy, a small kingdom located to the northeast of Lavodoria. Fashioned from silver, it's of even flatness and the edge is well-rounded. The obverse depicts an ugly-looking ruler. The reverse has a large "1" above "guilder". You can carry 5000.
