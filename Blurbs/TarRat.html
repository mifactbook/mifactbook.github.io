--- layout: creatures title: Tar Rat mob_id: 136 ---

Often found in and around Tar Pits, this 5-8 hand long, tar-encrusted rodent is ever-ready to remove an intruder with its unusually long teeth. Chomp, chomp. Nibble, nibble. Chew. It is excellent at surprising a foe because it has somehow learned how to live in the air-pockets of the Tar Pits. Bash away to win the day.
