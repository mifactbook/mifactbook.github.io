This Voodoo elixir cures all sorts of lesser poisons quickly. Into a Cauldron goes 21 Purple Lotus Leaves, 1 White Lotus Leaf and 3 Oil. Cover and simmer for 15 Action Points. To do this write a MAKE order: M 20. You'll get 7 batches of the elixir. To USE/CONSUME, write: U 20. There's no limit to the amount of elixir you may carry.
