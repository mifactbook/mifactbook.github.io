Unreadable Scroll

This is a rolled-up scroll of rahnhide. Upon unrolling it, large, strange, undecipherable symbols are revealed. There is a big gap between the large line of symbols at the top (a title?) and the smaller symbols beneath. You once heard a tale of how ancient wizards stored spells on magical scrolls - this must be one. You can carry 10.
