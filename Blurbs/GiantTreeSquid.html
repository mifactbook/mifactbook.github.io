--- layout: creatures title: Giant Tree Squid mob_id: 222 loc: - name: "Giant Tree Squid Nest" ---

Watch out! Not all those vines you pass by in Jungle terrain are really vines - some of them are the long dark tentacles of the Giant Tree Squid. This arboreal menace lies in wait to grab the next passer by and make a meal of them. Its tentacles are very strong and will attempt to lift you up towards its snapping beak. You'd better have an edged weapon ready, or else!
