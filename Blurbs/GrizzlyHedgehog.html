Large. Brown. Savage paws. Deadly muzzle. Mad. Mean. Tenacious. Surprisingly fast. Sticks denizen. The Grizzly Hedgehog. No relation to Arnold Schwarzenegger. It takes what it wants. It kills when it feels like it. You've crossed its path. If you survived once, will you again?
