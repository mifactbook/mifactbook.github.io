--- layout: items item_name: Large Clam Shell item_id: 27 ---

These are found right where they should be - in any terrain that's adjacent to the sea. They are useful for digging. You should be able to dig deep enough to make a Hidey-hole, but probably not enough to make a Deep Pit. You may carry three shells. A Large Clam Shell only lasts for one dig as it usually breaks when rocky soil is encountered. You will automatically discard your broken clam shell.
