--- layout: creatures title: Log Hog mob_id: 347 ---

An enormous member of the rodent clan of Monster Island, the Log Hog is a large-bellied creature with long chewing teeth, sharp digging claws, and a foul disposition. They prefer jungle terrain over anything else but will, in a pinch, take up residence in a fallen timber just about anywhere. so be careful when you take a rest by sitting on a log. It may be home to someone (or something) that will not appreciate the company.
