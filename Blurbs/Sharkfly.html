--- layout: creatures title: Sharkfly mob_id: 182 ---

If you're just plodding along in swampy or Sticks terrain and find yourself ferociously attacked by a small bird, an opportunistic Sharkfly has found you. It wants you for dinner. It has a very small brain and doesn't doesn't realise that you're more than it can handle. So what if it is the smallest bird on the Island - that its advantage. Trying to hit it is like trying to swat mosquitoes with a 2x4. Naturally, it comes complete with an amazing set of chompers. Yowzer!
