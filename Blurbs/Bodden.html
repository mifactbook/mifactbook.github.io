--- layout: blurbs title: Boddens blurb: |

The clannish Boddens are the denizens of High Jungle terrain. They're quite tall and very bony-looking. Friendly they're not, and they're quick to demonstrate their accuracy with Blowpipe darts. Boddens also have a flair with Whip weaponry. They dress in a leafy covering that doesn't look like it provides any useful protection. If you find that you can't get to a certain square in High Jungle terrain (no vines going into it), you've located a square in which one of the Bodden Clans live. Which is not necessarily a good thing.
