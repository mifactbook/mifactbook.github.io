Conserving Food

No, this is not the Richard Simmons' Deal-A-Meal. Dieting is a special discipline you can learn at an Inn. Conserving foold can be very useful if you are planning on entering an area where the hunting and foraging is known to be poor. Use the DIET order: DI, to begin your diet. Issue it again to stop your diet. This order costs no Action Points, but limits your Friendliness to no better than 3 (slightly unfriendly). It also does not prevent you from feasting (if you've more than 12 Food) during MAKE CAMP.
