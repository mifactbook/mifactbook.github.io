--- layout: items item_name: Damaged Black Iron Helm item_id: 401 ---

Like its less colorful counterpart, the Damaged Black Iron Helm is a worthy, though damaged, piece of armor. Its flat black finish makes it the perfect choice for those wishing to go silently in the night. A good Forger could likely fix it up as good as new. It is an Armor Class 2 item and you can carry 2.
