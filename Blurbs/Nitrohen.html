Pure red and resembling a 50lb chicken with a shark-like mouth, this large egg-laying fowl is most often found in sticks terrain, nesting in a clump of bamboo. These birds are fiercely protective of their nests and will attack anything that comes near. As Nitrohens charge at their foe, using a pole weapon to skewer the bird upon has the most effect. The nest of a Nitrohen may contain up to 3 eggs.
