Ah, the wonderful Calabash tree that's native to Sand & Palms terrain. You want 'em pipe? You smoke 'em to relax? This 'em the item your want. To make one, have a knife and be at a square having a Calabash Tree. Issue the MAKE order: M 40. It takes 8 Action Points to make one and you can carry 2. Once you have a pipe you'll want to find some Umber Grass.
