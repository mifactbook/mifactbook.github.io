Back in the reign of Jut Goddab, the tenth Lavodorian King, the Royal Guard complained about their greaves. They just didn't measure up to the rest of their armour. So, Jut Goddab commissioned his Magesmiths to make a set of special leggings. They succeeded. The addition of four spikelets sticking out along the front at a 45 degree angle, gives these leggings an offensive role in battle. Constructed from a blending of Iron, Gold and Kashart, the leggings are thought to be unbreakable. They provide Armour Class 4 protection (as greaves). In addition, sometimes the spikelets will cause extra dmage to a foe during battle, particularly when wrestling. You can carry 1 pair.
