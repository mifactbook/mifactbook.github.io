--- layout: items item_name: Bigschnozz Charm item_id: 119 ---

When equipped, this unusual item makes a Bignose's nose look even more frightening. The charm's magical effect is to confuse a monster making a Snatch attempt on you - it decreases the chance of a successful snatch. Only a Bignose would wear such a ghastly charm - other races cannot even equip it. You can carry 2.
