This is identical to the Jute Rope which you automatically collect from Jute Vines in Swamp terrain. It is an essential piece of equipment if you wish to enter High Jungle terrain. It's also of help when you fall into quicksand. Plus there's a bunch more ways it can be useful. You'll collect a Spare Rope when you've reached a certain level of Muscleness. You can carry just one Spare Rope.
