This item is a symbol of identity to a Loggerhead. It's made of all sorts of knickknacks: A stray (damaged) Bit Coin, a hollow hoop, a Hawk Buzzard feather, etc. It's smelly and not without some crawling insects. As a Loggerhead plunges through life he'll add and add to it. If you kill a Loggerhead you'll take this necklace - a Jossman Bounty item. You can carry 6.
