--- layout: creatures title: Sunbasker mob_id: 145 ---

This rusty leaf-coloured lizard has a very big mouth. It's always on the lookout for an easy meal. As it attacks with its mouth, a distracting "gwopple" sound is emitted. The Sunbasker is found darting in and around coastal areas. It's pretty easy to defeat in battle. Its tail is a tasty morsel. Club it!
