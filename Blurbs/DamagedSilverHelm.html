--- layout: items item_name: Damaged Silver Helm item_id: 313 ---

This helm has two big cracks in it and will occasionally slip off your head. Still, it provides Armour Class 1 protection. Better yet, have it repaired by a Forger because an undamaged Silver Helm provides Armour Class 2 protection. Because it is crafted from silver, it provides additional protection against certain foes. You can carry two of them.
