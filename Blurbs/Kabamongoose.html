--- layout: creatures title: Kabamongoose mob_id: 191 ---

The Kabamongoose is one of the swiftest creatures of the island. Zip, zip. When this good-sized Creature isn't busy hunt- ing snakes, it hunts bigger prey. It's sort of fearless. And stupid. Render it senseless (or worse) with an Edged weapon. The Kabamongoose is off-white in color, with some gray speckles, and is most often found in bamboo-laden terrain.
