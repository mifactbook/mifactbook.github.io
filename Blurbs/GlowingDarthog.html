--- layout: creatures title: Glowing Darthog mob_id: 375 ---

The Glowing Darthog is a creature of the day by necessity. It's not the biggest creature around but it is unique. It gives off an unearthly green glow bright enough to light up a room. This, of course, makes it an easy target for the nocturnal predators of Monster Island. For you, a day time Ruk, it just means its a curiosity that can bite your head off.
