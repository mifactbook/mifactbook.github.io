Slash, hack and thrust home! This gleaming Long Sword is well-balanced and finely made. The Light Sword is the bane of Followers of the Dark. It is a Damage Class 4 Edged weapon but when wielded agains Monsters and Creatures of the Dark its damage is doubled. It can only be equipped by a Follower of the Light whose muscle is at least 110. You can carry 1.
