Larger cousin to the Giant Tortoise, the Snapping Turtle can be best described as attitude on the half-shell. Always in a nasty temper, the Snapping Turtle will attempt to bite anything that moves in its vicinity. Its muscular jaws are strong enough to snap bone in a single bite. A pole weapon might be best to keep this unpleasant creature at a distance until you kill it.
