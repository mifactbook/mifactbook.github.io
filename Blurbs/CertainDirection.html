A Ruk's sense of direction is notoriously poor. If unguided, a Ruk might just get led astray. If you find yourself in a spot where you might get lost, you can always rely on a Swinging Ball and be called names by your friends and enemies or you can come equipped with CERTAIN DIRECTION which will never lead you astray. Since it is a situational spell, you need not issue an order to cast it, but it will cost 3 Spell Points whenever you use it.
