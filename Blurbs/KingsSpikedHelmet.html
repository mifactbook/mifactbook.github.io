--- layout: items item_name: King's Spiked Helmet item_id: 349 ---

Several hundred years ago, Gon Tonok, the fourth Lavodorian King, had his Magesmiths produce a helmet for his Royal Guard. Five spikelets stick out around the sides of the helm, giving it a fearsome look. Constructed from a blending of Iron, Gold and Kashart, the helm is nearly unbreakable. This helmet provides Armour Class 4 protection. Sometimes your spikes will cause additional damage to a foe. You can carry 1.
