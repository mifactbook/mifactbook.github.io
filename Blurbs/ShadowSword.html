--- layout: items item_name: Shadow Sword item_id: 318 ---

This dark-coloured, well balanced Fuvite Long Sword is difficult to see, enabling it to hit home with great damage. Normally it is a Damage Class 4 Edged weapon, but when wielded against Monsters and creatures who are favoured by a god other than Fuvah, its damage is doubled. It can only be equipped by a Fuvite whose Muscle is at least 110. You can carry 1.
