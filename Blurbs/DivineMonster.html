This advanced Disciple spell is used to divine information about the location, Health, Spell Points, and perhaps more, of a nearby spellcasting Monster. Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 71 (Monster #). Actual success/degree of success depends on the distance between you and your target, and, both of yours religion, site/location, and spellcasting ability. The spell is pretty reliable within 24 squares of your target. The maximum range is about 40 squares.
