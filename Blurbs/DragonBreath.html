Followers of Kadar

Followers of the Dragon Demigod, Kadar the Black, have been infused with the spirit of his children. During battle, his followers are granted the ability of Dragon Breath. This potent spell can take several forms dependent on the closest Dragon Spirit at the time of its casting. The Silver Dragon grants a freezing cone of frost, the Fire Drake gives a flaming breath, and the Gold Dragon a sonic blast in the form of its Dragon Roar. This powerful spell has crippled many opponents in countless battles. To use this spell, issue the CAST SPELL ORDER: C 977. It uses 15 Spell Points each time it is cast.
