--- layout: creatures title: Seagroat mob_id: 155 ---

Barely fluttering over the ground this avian is really strange to watch. It hovers from spot to spot, like a low-flying hummingbird. But the Seagroat resembles a flaming yellow whooping crane, with a home not too far from the sea. A club can turn one into a yummy meal (seafood au groatin?) - and rather quickly too.
