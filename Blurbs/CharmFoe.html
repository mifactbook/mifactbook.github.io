Some Ruks are just plain unfriendly. You buy 'em a mug of grog and they still turn around and try to chop off your head the first time they see you alone in the jungle. Fear not, for those who hate having enemies, just issue the CHARM FOE spell as your defensive battle spell. The good vibes you cast will save you from all but the most determined of foes. It will cost you 50 Spell Points each time you cast it, and if you're really nice, you may actually make a friend or two.
