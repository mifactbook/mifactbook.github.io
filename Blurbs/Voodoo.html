Voodoo

Voodoo is a means to manipulate the forces of nature for a purpose. Using Voodoo is usually a three-step process. First, you must acquire the knowledge of making a Voodoo concoction. Second, combine the ingredients in your Voodoo Cauldron. This commonly requires you to issue a MAKE order and takes a varying number of Action Points to produce the Voodoo item, usually an elixir. Third, to use the elixir you will most likely issue the USE/CONSUME order. However, some Voodoo'd items are used in battle and you need to EQUIP these.

Note: There is something known as Black Voodoo. This is a very exotic and dangerous form of Voodoo, and requires special knowledge and skills to fool around with. Fool if you do!
