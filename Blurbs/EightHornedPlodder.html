--- layout: creatures title: Eight-Horned Plodder mob_id: 328 ---

The Eight-Horn Plodder is a feral relative of the common Plodder, commonly used as a mount. The Eight-horn Plodder is not, however, suitable for a mount. It is more suitable as a thoracic ventilator as its full-speed rushes are designed to puncture your chest leaving eight robust-sized holes. If you are quick, you may be able to dodge to the side and take a few swipes at it with your weapon. If not, someone will be able to use you to water plants after you take a drink of water.
