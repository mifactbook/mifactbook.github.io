Reprint Orders

Reprint Creature Blurb

If you should ever wish to review a creature blurb, it is a simple task. Issue the REPRINT CREATURE BLURB order: KA Creature# where the Creature# is the number of the creature you wish to reprint. It will cost you zero action points to perform this task.

Reprint Item Blurb

Similar to the Reprint Creature Blurb, the REPRINT ITEM BLURB is simple to work. Issue the order: KI Item# where the Item# is the number of the item you wish to reprint the blurb. It will then print out on your turn. It costs no action points to use.

Reprint Spell Blurb

If you ever find yourself forgetting the basics of a spell, issue the REPRINT SPELL BLURB: KS Spell# where Spell# is the number of the spell you wish to reprint. It will cost you no action points for this order.

Reprint Miscellaneous Blurb

A handy order if you ever forget the details of some vague blurb that you have misplaced. Issue the REPRINT MISC. BLURB order: KB Blurb# where Blurb# is the number referencing the blurb as it appears from your LIST BLURBS order.
