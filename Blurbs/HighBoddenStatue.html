This crafted Talisman shows the true genius of the Bodden race. Once the 13th Blackbone Ring is placed over the three posts, the rings lock into place and the Statue transforms from a Bodden to a High Bodden. Each High Bodden statue varies based on the individual Rings used in its construction. Yours appears to have a number of small flaws probaly due to the fact that the Bodden you killed for the rings came from many different families. A true statue formed by the Ancestor Rings of a single family would be a priceless treasure. This Talisman would be treasured by any God as an addition to his Treasure Room. Taking the rings back off the statue is a feat beyond your mental capacity. You can carry only one of these valuable items. More would probably get you killed.
