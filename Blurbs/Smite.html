The Smite is a large, ferocious oinker with one prominent feature - a long spear- like horn extending forward from above its nose. This horn runs from 16 to 20 hands long. The Smite can gut a foe before its foe can get near enough to do it harm. The Smite rarely ventures beyond the open terrain of Scrubland and Sand & Palms, as its horn makes movement difficult, if not impossible, in denser terrain. It is best to fight fire with fire and battle a Smite with a Pole weapon.
