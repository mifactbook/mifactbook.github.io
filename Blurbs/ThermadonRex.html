Yes, this is the large-sized Thermadon. This one comes with wings and is about 3 times bigger. It uses claws and sharp wings in an attempt to shred a foe into a bloody heap. Thermadon Rex are also covered with sharp scales which can cut an unprotected Monster quite nicely. While no match for a Dragon, the Thermadon Rex is deadly enough that even a Dragon will choose to go around its territory.
