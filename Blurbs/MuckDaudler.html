--- layout: creatures title: Muck Daudler mob_id: 134 ---

Often seen sliding over the surface of swampy muck is the Muck Daudler. Eight hands long, it propels itself using turtle-like flippers that are rounded and enable it to stay on the surface of ponds, marshes etc. Its two front flippers are equipped with a stinger. Missile Weapons are the weapon of choice. If you're in a Raft when it finds you, adios muchachos! One good thing is that killing one will net you a small feast o' muck.
