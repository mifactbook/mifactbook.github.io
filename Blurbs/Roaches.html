Roaches

Eeeeewwwww!!! What's slimey, nasty, smelly, and guaranteed to give you a bad case of the Heebie-Jeebies? Roaches. They seem to proliferate anywhere there are dead monsters. Worst of all, there are tons of different kinds from small nuisances, like the Common Cave Roach to giant behemoths better left unmentioned that can bite a monster in half and swallow the pieces in a single gulp. Be warned, some Roaches are never alone. Little could be worse than being hunted by a Roach Pack.
