You're in Sticks terrain and you hear an Oink? OINK! Stay out of the path of this marauding swine. Protruding from its fat head are fifteen horn-like, hand-long spikes. And it likes to see just how many times they can impact on a foe's face. How nice. Stab this red brick-colored bandit with a Pointed weapon.
