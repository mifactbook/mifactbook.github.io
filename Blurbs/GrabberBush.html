When you're looking for a spot to sit down in, take care that you don't rest near a Grabber Bush. Not actually a plant at all, this creature will try to grab you with its tentacles, which look like a bush's branches, and drive you onto its sharp thorns. After killing you, it will slowly digest your corpse. A sharp Edged weapon is its nemesis. If you want to make sure you never meet another, stick to Sand & Palms terrain; they're never found there.
