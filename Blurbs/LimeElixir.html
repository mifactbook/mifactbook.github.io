Lime Elixir - the Voodoo Master's version of Gatorade. Just down a Waterskin of Lime Elixir and spend the next five or so weeks not worrying about water. Your thirst will be quenched by this highly nutritious concoction. This elixir is quite useful when exploring an area where water is scarce. Use the MAKE order: M 64 for 9 Action Points to produce Lime Elixir. You'll need 4 Limes, 1 Coconut, 2 oil, and a Filled Waterskin. Combine these ingredients in your Voodoo Cauldron to obtain one Lime Elixir. You can carry 2 batches. Write a USE/CONSUME order: U 64. Note that the Waterskin used to hold the elixir can be recovered as an empty Waterskin.
