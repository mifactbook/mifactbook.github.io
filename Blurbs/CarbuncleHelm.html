So you want to win friends and influence enemies? Try the unusual Carbuncle Helm. Expend 20 Action Points to carefully clean out a Carbuncle Shell, then firmly adhere a soft Toad Leather to its interior using a Waxed Ooze. You can cut the Toad Leather with a knife to make a secure chin strap to hold it on your head. This helm will provide you with Class 3 Protection for your head. You can carry two.
