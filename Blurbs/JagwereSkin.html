Killing a Jagwere gets you its skin. You will be acquiring a leather that's tough as well as beautiful, perfect for a stylish haversuit. At a Far Inn you may have this skin made into a haversuit that will never need major repair. Issue the USE order: U 167 - the Far Inn in will charge you 12 Oculars (There is no Action Pt cost.) You can carry 1 skin.
