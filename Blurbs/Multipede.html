Ten hands long, black as night, equipped with poisonous glands the length of its body. Its body broken into a thousand segments, each one bearing four-clawed legs. Meet the Multipede, a resident of the dark where it feeds on the dregs of matter that enter its dark realm. If you invade this realm you may find it has developed a taste for living Ruk. Beware the sound of its multi-feet in the dark as it means you have seen the last of the sunlight and have met your doom.
