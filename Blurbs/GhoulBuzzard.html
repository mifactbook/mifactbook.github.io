--- layout: creatures title: Ghoul Buzzard mob_id: 128 ---

Some call this buzzard "Shroud's Pet" because of its incredibly evil appearance. The fact that it is a scavenger of dead animals adds to its fearsome reputation. Ghoul Buzzards seem to sense when death is nearby. It is uncommon for more than one to appear at a time. They are so vicious they even attack each other. From time to time, when they are hungry enough, a Ghoul Buzzard will attack another living being. However it will not eat the fresh meat, leaving the flesh to decay for a few days before feasting. Capturing a Ghoul Buzzard is not easy.
