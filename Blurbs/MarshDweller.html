--- layout: creatures title: Marsh Dweller mob_id: 135 ---

Often hidden in stinky swamps and marshes is this very unfriendly Creature. It launches tendrils that resemble a chain and grappling hook. These snag you and slowly retract, carrying you toward its very large mouth. We're talking about a mouth that's almost bigger than the Creature. It's obvious it can eat most things whole, including you. A Pole Weapon might be most practical against it.
