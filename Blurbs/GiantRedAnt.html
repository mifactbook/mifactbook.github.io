--- layout: creatures title: Giant Red Ant mob_id: 403 loc: - name: "Bodden Camp" ---

The Giant Red Ant is very similar in appearance to the smaller Giant Black Ant. The biggest difference besides colouring, is the terrible burning senstation that accompanies it's bite. At 5 hands in length, this is not a creature you really want to mess with. A Pole Class weapon may have a chance to penetrate the joints in its body and will provide some chance to keep it off you.
