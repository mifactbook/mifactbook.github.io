This large, swift, yellow-spotted, black snake is found in Jungle terrain and the occasional dark place. Hack it to death instantly before its yucky goo can muck things up. Its goo eats through organic things like Carved Clubs, Blow Pipes, and Squzskin Gauntlets. The Goo Snake is relatively rare. But not rare enough.
