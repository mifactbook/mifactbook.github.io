So some evil monster has poisoned your favourite waterhole, or perhaps even been so uncivilised as to bathe in it. This spell is just what you need. Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 19. It takes 20 Action Points and 10 Spell points each time it is cast. This will improve the quality of the water in the waterhole or pond located in your current square.
