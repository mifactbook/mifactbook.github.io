--- layout: items item_name: Dragonbone Sword item_id: 326 ---

This magical, fantastic great sword feels light and nimble, but packs a tremendous wallop. Swish! It's solid Dragonbone, which is off white in colour, and features a black, leather-wrapped hilt. The Dragonbone Sword is a Damage Class 5 Edged weapon. What's even better is that in battle it's like fighting with two extra Skill Levels in Edged Weapons, but only if it happens to be the weapon you're equipped with when going into battle. To equip it you must have at least 110 Muscle. You can carry 1.
