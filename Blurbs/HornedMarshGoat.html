--- layout: creatures title: Horned Marsh Goat mob_id: 186 ---

In swampy terrain it is not uncommon to come across a pair of these Creatures butting heads playfully. This bloated-looking goat is forever chomping reeds and anything else that looks alive. This is an indifferent Creature; you don't bother it and it don't bother you. It's got a brown, grainy, thick skin. Tufts of white hair mark its legs distinctively. Kabuki favours this creature.
