This advanced Disciple spell summons a creature! Right to you! Sounds great, huh? It might be. Or it might be a disaster! You see, who knows if the Creature will be friendly, hungry or wildly irritated? Or be some abomination that'll gulp you down in one bite? Which is what makes this spell just the sort for thrill-seeking idiot spellcasters. But then again, you never know - maybe something good will be summoned. Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 1. It takes 60 Action Points. Where this spell is cast, luck, and how many Spell Points you have available determines what Creature is summoned. Whatever is summoned determines how many Spell Points are expended.

Warning: Jossmen and Bodden have been known to decapitate a monster who summons a creature into a square where they reside.
