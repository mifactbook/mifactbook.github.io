Battle Cry

When entering hand-to-hand battle, screaming out a Battle Cry can temporarily stun an opponent. This results in your opponent missing his/her/its first chance at swinging/hacking at you.

You have three choices of BATTLE CRY orders:

  • B1 - has you scream your race's Battle Cry.
  • B5 - has you scream your Group's Battle Cry (if your Group has one).
  • B9 - has you scream your God's Battle Cry (if you're a follower of a God).

This is a Standing Order - issue the identical order to cancel.
