The skin of a Gator is known for its toughness and can make a fine suit of body armor. To make it you'll need four Gator Hides, a Knife, and you must be in a square having a Jute vine. Issue the MAKE order M 6 and it takes 42 Action Points to fashion. You may possess only one set of Gator Armor. The tough skin of the Gator makes this item Armor Class 4.
