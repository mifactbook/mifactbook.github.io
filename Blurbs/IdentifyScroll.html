By casting this spell on an Unreadable Scroll that you posess, it alters the scroll in such a way that you can now read its title. To cast the spell issue the CAST SPELL order: C 24. This spell takes 8 Action Points of heady concentration and uses up 5 Spell Points. If you posess more than one Unreadable Scroll, you'll want to cast this spell once for each one you posess. Most scroll titles are the name of a spell.

Note: Spell #25, Read Scroll, is needed to read the contents of a scroll.
