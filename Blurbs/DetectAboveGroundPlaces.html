This spell allows you to detect the presence of Far Structures, Forts, Towers, Large Camps, Tombs, Graveyards, Crypts and more. You'll detect ones that are within two squares of you in all directions (covers a 5x5 area of squares) Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 88. It takes 24 Action Points and uses 12 Spell Points.

Note: Temples and Dark places are not detected.
