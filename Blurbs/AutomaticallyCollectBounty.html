--- layout: blurbs title: Automatically Collect Bounty blurb: |

Most experienced travelers are familiar the hustle and bustle of Far Inns. Things are so busy when you check in that you often forget to try to collect your bounty from those misbegotten Knolltir, Loggerheads, and Mine Trolls that you dispatched during your travels. Worse yet, you are stuck with carry all the stuff around till you can find another Inn. The solution to this dilemma is to ask the Innkeeper about bounties as soon as you see him. Jossmen will gladly oblige you immediately and give you your oculars. From now on, you will do this every time you come to an Inn. No other order is necessary. You clever devil.
