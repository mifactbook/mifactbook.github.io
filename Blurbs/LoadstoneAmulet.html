Normans who are exercising maniacs find this magical Amulet quite beneficial. If the Norman wearing this very attractive looking Amulet Jazzercises at least once uring his turn, in Make Camp he'll regain up to 8 Health. Health is only regained if his ending Health is lower than his starting health for the Turn. Only a Norman knows how to use this Amulet properly - other races cannot even equip it. You can carry 2.
