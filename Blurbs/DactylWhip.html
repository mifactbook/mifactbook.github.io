You have 30 Action Points and three Dactyl Hides? Great! Use the MAKE order: M 8, and you'll have yourself a Dactyl Whip. It's a Whip weapon of Damage Class 3. The Dactyl Whip is particularly useful against thin-skinned Creatures. You can carry only one Dactyl Whip and you do need a Knife to make it.
