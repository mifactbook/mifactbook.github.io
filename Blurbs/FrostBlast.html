Mage Spell

This spell creats a cone of intense cold which expands out and ingulfs its target. The freezing nature of this spell has left many opponents as literal statues near dead and yet unable to defend themselves as they stand and wait for the final blow to end their existence. This spell has an interesting interaction with Fire Shields. The flame resistant nature of the shield appears to intensify the damage from this spell. To cast this spell in battle, issue the Cast Spell order: C 907. It uses 15 points each time it is used.
