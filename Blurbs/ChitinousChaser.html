What could be worse than a giant slug? How about a giant, armoured, carniverous slug with an attitude! The Chitinous Chaser is all that and fast to boot. About 20 hands long, this critter is covered with large chitinous belts, and wields a single appendage that ends in a spiked ball. A Pole weapon would be the best means of attack.
