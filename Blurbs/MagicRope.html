Up ahead in the roiling jungle you see your prey. Its a huge scaly beast that you've always wanted to catch. "Gorgeous!" You take out your trusty Jute Rope...oops, it looks like you lost it so you take out your trusty Spare Rope...oops, looks like you lost that, too. Drat. You've missed your chance. Well, fear not. Now, with the amazing "MAGIC ROPE" spell, you'll never lack for a rope of any kind again. If you have the need, you'll just cast this situation spell at a cost of 3 Spell Points and be on your merry way. By Crikey!

Note: The magic rope can also be used to enter High Jungle and Fungus Forests and to set traps. Unfortunately it won't last forever, so it cannot be used to harness a mount.
