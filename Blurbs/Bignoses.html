--- layout: blurbs title: Bignoses blurb: |

Because they are fairly ugly and above average in size, Bignoses think they are the best. And who's to argue? After all, they do seem to be superior at battle, particularly in Sticks terrain. They're also quite adept at building muscle. Their prominent proboscises assist them in finding exotic-smelling things and places. The downside? They have a hard time noticing trapped pits, holes, and the like.
