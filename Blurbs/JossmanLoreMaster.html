Jossman Lore Master

You'll recognize a Lore Master by his loden green tunic and his knowing smile. Mounted on a trained Plodder, a Lore Master rides slowly and purposefully, studying Creatures and Plants. Some of the Far Inns located in the Starth region have a resident Lore Master who teaches special skills to visitors with coin and whatever prerequisites are required. (Only skills you have the prerequisites for are offered to you.) Issue the INN KNOWLEDGE order. Each of these special skills takes 80 Action Points to learn.
