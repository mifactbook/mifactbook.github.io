Balloon Ride

Now that most of the Dragons have been cleared from the air above Zargnoth, the Kwando Aviators have restarted their balloon shuttle service. First amass 500 Oculars worth of coinage, then go 8 squares east of a Far Inn near the Kunbar - Starth border and issue the new yell order: Y 3, "Air Kwando Flight Number 47 Cleared for Landing." A Kwando Aviator will land his balloon beside you. Upon payment of the 500 Oculars you'll be taken for a ride over the Crystal Hills. This takes 200 Action Points. Note: This is a one-way trip, so say goodbye to any travelling companions and stock up well for the journey, as you have no idea when or if you'll return. If you have a mount, you'll automatically abandoned it in departure square.
