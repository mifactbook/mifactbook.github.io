These are quite annoying! They are large, sturdy bugs found most often in Low Hills. If they decide they like you, they will follow you forever. Although the swarm will always be nearby, only one of them attacks at a time, usually just after you finish hunting and select a final campsite for the week. It's tricky to be rid of them. Each attacks with its nasty stinger.
