A great Shield is hard to find. You can try begging, borrowing or buying. But one of the best is made with your own hands, the Tortoise Shield. You need to have a Knife and you need to be able to expend a Squzskin, a Giant Tortoise Shell and 60 Action Points. The squzskin will be used to fashion the strap and the Knife cuts and trims everything properly. Use the MAKE order: M 44. A Tortoise Shield is an Armour Class 5 Defensive weapon and you can carry just one - if your muscle is at least 120.
