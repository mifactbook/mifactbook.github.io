Ribbit. Ribbit. Hop. Hop. SMACK! So goes life for the Leather-bellied Toad. An island eating machine, this little beastie is not above taking a smack or two out of your flesh. Its mad hopping can drive you nuts as you try to find a weapon to take it out.
