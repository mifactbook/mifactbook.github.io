--- layout: creatures title: Crocadowder mob_id: 184 ---

Here's a creature that loves to lurk in a menacing fashion in and around wet, swampy terrain. Picture a brown cow dressed as acrocodile with large teeth. It loves a good meal the two-legged kind. Its thick hide makes the Crocodowder seemingly impervious to most weaponry. However, you can try knocking its puny brain senseless with Bashing weapons. Killing one is a challenge and even if you're successful, its hide is too tough to retrieve and work into something useful.
