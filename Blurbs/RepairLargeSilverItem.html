Rather than paying a Forger to repair a large silver item, you can magically repair it yourself by casting this spell. (A large silver item is a silver item that you can carry only one). Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 51 (Item#). It takes 7 Action Points and uses 10 Spell Points.
