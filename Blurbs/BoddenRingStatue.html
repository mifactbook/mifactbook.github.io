The Bodden Ring Statue is one of an incredible piece of artwork. It depicts a very muscular Bodden seated on the ground. The Bodden holds a large post in each hand and has a third post carefully balanced between his feet. The spacing between the statue and each post is very small and very precise. Almost as if this statue was meant to serve a purpose. Rumor has it these statues are closely linked with the tracking of Bodden family lines. But only a Bodden is likely to know how. This beautiful statue would be a welcome adition to any Treasure Room. You can carry but one.
