This hunchbacked simian predator lurks in hilly and Sticks terrain. Approaching prey gets knocked over by a sudden blast of air from its powerful lungs, after which the Huffinpuffer leaps out to finish the job. These creatures are cunning and fairly strong, and only the best wrestling tactics will stop one from twisting a monster into a nice pretzel shape. Its humped back makes it almost impossible to pin.
