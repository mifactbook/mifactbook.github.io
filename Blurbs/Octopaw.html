What's worse than a Tentaculus armed with incredibly strong gripping tentacles or a Double-Pawed Cougar with huge sharp claws? Try this Mutate that is a cross between the two! This squid-like being is armed with eight huge tentacles capable of pulling a monster limb from limb. But worse yet, each tentacle ends in a huge furry paw and eight razor sharp claws. Top it off with a mouth full of feline teeth and you have the horror that is the Octopaw. To kill one, you'd better work on your stab-and-move. Otherwise you'll be so much kitty-litter...or is that squid food?
