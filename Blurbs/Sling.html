--- layout: items item_name: Sling item_id: 46 ---

Littering most squares are animal carcasses. Besides contributing a foul stench to the air, these cadavers are picked over by the island's many scavengers. You notice one of these cadavers nearby and poke at it with a stick. The idea occurs to you that if you could find a larger one you could make a Sling from its hide. Issue the MAKE order: M 46 to make a Sling. It takes 22 Action Points as it takes awhile to successfully locate a proper carcass. A Sling is a Missile Weapon of Damage Class 1. You may carry just two.
