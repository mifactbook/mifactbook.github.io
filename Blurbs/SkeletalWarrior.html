Not the hackneyed pile of bones of a Skeleton, the skeletal Warrior was created by a novice Mage from the bleached bones of a dead warrior. They are usually equipped with remnant Black Iron Gauntlets which they wore in a previous life. This makes them a little tougher to bash to pieces and, if you look closely into their eye sockets, you might even see a hint of intelligence. This makes for a scarey foe.
