Fuvah Disciple/Priest

This offensive battle spell is cast by a Fuvah Disciple or Priest against opponents in the hope of temporarily disorientationg them. If it overcomes their magical resistance, they'll be at a severe disadvantage in the first round of melee fighting (and that includes them being unable to flee before the first round). Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 905. It uses up 12 Spell Points each time it is cast.
