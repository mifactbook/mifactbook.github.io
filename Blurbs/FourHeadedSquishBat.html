--- layout: creatures title: Four-headed Squish Bat mob_id: 373 ---

Not as big as a Spladder Moth but certainly big enough to smoosh the average Ruk flat as a pancake, the 4-headed Squish bat is a hideously deformed monstrosity that comes equipped with two sets of leathery wings, four heads, eight jaws, and 13 taloned legs. Not your ordinary average beast. Zoingots swear they had nothing to do with the making of this one. How it came into being is anyone's guess. Just be careful in Wasteland terrain so as not to get, well, squished.
