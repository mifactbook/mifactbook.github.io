Ants.... No big deal right... Little bugs you squish for entertainment. If you have gone without food for a while, you even use them for a snack. Now this Ant will make a meal of you. Over 4 hands long (half of which are those wicked pinchers), this bubba is not likely to be on your menu. Giant Black Ants are found only in High Jungle or its remains. They live in middle canopy levels. They are best avoided, but should you need to fight one, a Pole Class weapon is best. You may be to penetrate the joints in the Ants armor. If nothing else, it keeps the darn thing at a distance.
