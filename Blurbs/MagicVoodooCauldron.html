Some cauldrons are good and some are just a waste of space. If you are tired of lugging around your cauldron, then this spell may be of use. As a situational spell, any time you've need for a Cauldron and don't have one, you'll adeptly conjure one up in your mind and do what needs to be done. This will cost you 9 spell Points every time you need it.
