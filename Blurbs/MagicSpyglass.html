T he Spyglass is one of the most important things you can have on Monster Island. Of course, it is bulky and takes up a lot of room. With the situational spell, MAGIC SPYGLASS, you can magically train your sight to the same accuity as a Spyglass thereby rendering your Spyglass a luxury you need not keep around. Best of all, this spell does not cost any Spell Points to use.
