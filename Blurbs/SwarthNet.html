--- layout: items item_name: Swarth Net item_id: 37 ---

This is used both for fishing from a raft and to entangle an opponent in battle. These nets are incredibly strong and durable. This item is hand-crafted by Jossmen and for sale at some of their Far Inns. You can carry one net. It is an Armor Class 2 Defensive Weapon. What makes this net even more useful is that it happens to be perfect for capturing some creatures...except for a few that'll rip it to shreds (and perhaps you with it).
