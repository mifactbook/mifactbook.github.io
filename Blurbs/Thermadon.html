--- layout: creatures title: Thermadon mob_id: 148 ---

Living in the Sticks, this mid-sized lizard is a danger to all who pass within range of its nose. It particularly dislikes hunters and gatherers. About 8 hands in length, on rare occasions it attacks with its claws. Usually, it slashes at you with its tail. Where you find one Thermadon, you'll find plenty more nearby.
