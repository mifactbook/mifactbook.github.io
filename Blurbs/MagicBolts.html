Conjurer Spell

This combat spell is a favorite for many senior disciples. It brings into being small extremely accurate darts of pure magic force. The darts themselves are not incredibly powerful, but the number of darts generated increases as the caster grows in power. For every 3 Temples that the Caster has visited, an additional bolt will be generated (i.e., 2 darts after visiting 6 temples; 3 darts after visiting 9 temples). At 8 spell points per use, this spell is very efficient for any level caster. To use this Spell in battle, issue the Cast Spell order: C 955.
