--- layout: creatures title: Wolfcur mob_id: 188 ---

The good news is that this is the least dangerous wolf of the island. The bad news is that its awesome jaws and knife-like teeth can rip you to shreds. Bashing its brains out is your only hope. It is most often found in Low Hills terrain and some- times has a companion nearby. The Wolfcur is obviously a descendant of a deranged(?) wolf-bear breeding experiment. Special note: This Creature is a minion of the Dark.
