--- layout: creatures title: Mondocanda mob_id: 297 ---

Forty hands long worth of coiled fury just waiting for a meal of kicking Ruk. This beast is even larger than a Great Trap Snake and has a meaner disposition to boot. If you want to continue your travels on the Island, you'd best equip with a good blade and slash to kill as quickly as possible. The only good thing about this snake is that it provides a very good meal for you and a friend.
