Spyglasses are great! They enable you to both discern the terrain of all squares one away and spot the larger structures (such as Large Caves and Far Inns) in these squares. To fabricate a Spyglass, issue the MAKE order: M 24. You need the right three parts: a Black Pack Rowt Lens, a Yogorilla Lens, and a Funnel Snakeskin. It takes 10 Action Points to make. You can carry just one and you'll use it whenever it's opportune.
