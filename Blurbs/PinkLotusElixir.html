So if you tire of the weak boost you get from White Lotus Elixir, maybe you need a shot of pink Lotus Elixer. It is easy to make. It you've got a voodoo Cauldon, a white Lotus Elixir, one Oil, and one Red Bauble, you'll spend 18 action points whipping up a batch, just use the MAKE Order: M 259 #, where # is the amount you wish to make. Note. You will not automatically consume these when your healthis low.
