--- layout: creatures title: Plodder mob_id: 175 ---

This is the most common Mount on the island due to its friendliness, low hunger (just 2 Food per turn), and relative easiness to train. Resembling a small zebra with fat, stubby legs, its vivid brown and red striping makes it easy to spot - which is quite handy for his owner. It moves slow 'n steady through most terrains, climbing and descending High Hills without hesitation. The Plodder is quiet, gentle and loyal. Although it has no offensive capability in battle, it's not a hindrance either. Some Creatures will be more reluctant to fight you when you ride this dependable Mount. Hey, it even responds to giddy-ups.
