Twelve hands long and full of piscine attitude, the Flying Ballyhoo is the toughest, orneriest fish a Ruk could ever hope to meet. Equipped with a pike-like bill and "scale"-mail armour, this beast is tough to meet in the water. To compound things, its long pectoral fins allow it to fly free of the water and their razor-sharp edges can cut you to ribbons. When it is through slicing and dicing, it will glide back to the water and slurp up the big pieces.
