Crawling in and about Tombs and Graveyards is this unfriendly critter, the Tomb Leech. It looks like a short-legged dog disguised as a giant leech. Its sucker is formidable. Using Edged Weapons is best as you really need to hack it to pieces to kill it. It moves silently; its dark color makes it hard to spot.
