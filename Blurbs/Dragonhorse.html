--- layout: creatures title: Dragonhorse mob_id: 290 ---

On first sight, you'll know you have witnessed something special. Few ever see a Dragonhorse as it only inhabits a select few dark places. It will seldom battle you as it is far too cunning to let you lay a weapon on its purple-hued flanks. But if you are an exceptional rider with at least a Riding Skill of 175, you may be able to capture and train it as a Mount. If you are so lucky, you will have the finest Mount ever made. Just be aware that Jossmen hold a natural fear of this beast as legend tells that Doom follows behind the rider of a Dragonhorse. Expect them to charge extra for stabling.
