Statue Carving

Wow! How utterly cool. With a little practice you can make your own statues! All it takes is a lot of time, a lot of patience and at least one piece of rare Hellwood. Once you've got your piece of Hellwood just settle down in a nice comfortable spot and expend 250 Action Points whittling and carving and etching and doodling. Depending on your skill, you might even be able to impress someone with it. Your actions are initiated by the Statue Carving Order SC. Note: This order is not yet programmed.
