Camoflaged in the Sticks, a Barkbrute can be nearly impossible to spot until it is too late. It's covered with sharp shards; these are its main weapon. It lumbers up to you on two strong legs and grabs onto your body..to crush you onto its shards. Pointed weapons seem best against this seven hand tall Creature.
