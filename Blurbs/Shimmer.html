--- layout: creatures title: Shimmer mob_id: 331 ---

If you are a little tired and suffering from a lack of water, a Shimmer may look more like a mirage in the desert than a dangerous foe. This creature is almost insubstantial and doesn't hit with a lot of power. But its lack of power is made up for by its overwhelming speed. Hitting one is not easy to do so you must maintain your composure, keep a steady eye, and swing like mad.
