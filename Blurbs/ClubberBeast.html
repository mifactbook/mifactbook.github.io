Another of the Leser Mutates, the Clubberbeast is a cross between a Tholomite and a Mandibular Crab. It will usually spring from a body of water and attempt to bash you to pieces with its large, oversized claw. Best to fight back quickly unless you are a fan of king-sizes headaches.
