Heal Self

No
Format
Spell Points
Action Points
16
C 16 [# SPs]
any
20

This spell helps you to quickly recover lost health. Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 16 (Spell Points). It take 20 Action Points. The more spell points allocated, the more health is recovered, at the rate of one point of health per spell point spent.

Note: If you try to allocate more Spell Points than you have health loss, the excess spell points will not be expended.
