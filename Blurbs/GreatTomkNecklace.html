--- layout: items item_name: Great Tomk Necklace item_id: 359 ---

Often the most dangerous Loggerheads are equipped with this Magical Amulet. It's very similar to a Tomk Necklace, but has a higher quality of knick knacks. What makes it special is its Magical ability to reduce damage received from most forms of Missile fire to almost nothing. It must be equipped for it to work.
