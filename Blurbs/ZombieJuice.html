So, you've decided to try raising the dead. Issue the MAKE order: M 67 and into your Voodoo Cauldron place 1 Dust of the Entombed, 1 White Lotus Leaf, 1 Tholomite Heart and 4 Oil. This takes 8 Action Points and makes one batch of Zombie Juice. To use it, move to a square having an Ancient Graveyard. If you've some type of digging implement you can issue the USE/CONSUME order: U 67. You'll exhume a corpse and apply an entire batch to its skin. In 4 Action Points, the corpse will begin to move. You've created a Zombie! It will follow (but not fight for you) until you issue the RELEASE CREATURE order: RC. It will then guard that square - attacking those who are not of you race/group who enter the square. You can carry 4 batches.
