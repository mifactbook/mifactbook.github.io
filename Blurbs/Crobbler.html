When in Jungle or Sand & Palms terrain, be on the lookout for a large, muddy brown, hairy vine. Chances are it's a Crobbler. Something that'll give you a good fight. Maybe two good fights. Maybe more. You have to be a superior wrestler to defeat this horrible growth.
