Every Monster likes something covering his head, offering some protection from an occasional enemy club. A Squzskin Helmet fits the bill, making one helluva fashion statement to boot! Just collect two Squzskins and using your Knife, work it for 30 Action Points using the MAKE order: M 49. This Helmet provides Armor Class 1 protection and you may carry two of them.
