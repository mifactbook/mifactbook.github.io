This snake has so many heads that fighting it just isn't fun. So you lop off a head or two; it still has plenty remaining to finish you off. Depressing. A great Edged weapon could make the difference between dinner for you and dinner for him. If you're a wimp, avoid Jungle and Swamp terrain and you'll be safe. Unless, that is, you wander into the wrong cave.
