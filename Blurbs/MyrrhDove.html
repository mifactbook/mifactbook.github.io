--- layout: creatures title: Myrrh Dove mob_id: 132 ---

If you've ever come across a sickeningly sweet smell while traveling in Low Hills you are probably passing by a Myrrh Dove nest. These birds are extremely beautiful to behold. Their feathers have a prismatic quality. When the sun strikes them they reflect in a variety of brilliant colors. Some believe the Myrrh Dove scent is hypnotic, because anyone who gazes at one for any length of time becomes overwhelmed with a sense of goodness and peace.
