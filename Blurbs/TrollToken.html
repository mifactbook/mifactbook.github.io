You are late for an appointment at the Exotica and suddenly find yourself without enough pocket change to get you and your mount across the Troll Bridge. It takes time to bash enough Loggerheads to gather the required coinage. You miss your appointment. Drat. If you've ever run into this situation, then you will be glad to know about Troll Tokens. Just give one to the local Troll Bridge Operator and you can go on your merry way. Quite the time save. You can carry up to six.
