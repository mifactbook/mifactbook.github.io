--- layout: creatures title: Thawk mob_id: 183 ---

The Thawk soars the skies of non-jungle terrains, always looking, looking, looking. Named after its loud, distinctive call, and resemblance to a hawk, this large navy blue avian is quite the aggressive pest. It preys on the smaller Creatures, but not exclusively. It's been known to remove a finger, hand, or eye from a Monster caught off-guard (perhaps Jazzercizing?). Of special note is that Thawks get along quite well with Loggerheads.
