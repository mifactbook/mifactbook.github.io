When delving into the coffins of a Tomb of Mumi, you can collect some Dust of the Entombed from the oldest coffins. This dust has several uses. The most common use is for making the exotic substance Zombie Juice. You can carry only one kitty of the dust. To carry more would be a great health risk, as this dust has very unusual properties. A few Monsters are allergc to it - if you begin sneezing uncontrollably, you'll have to drop this item.
