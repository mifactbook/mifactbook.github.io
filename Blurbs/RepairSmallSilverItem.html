Rather than paying a Forger to repair a small silver item, you can magically repair it yourself by casting this spell. (A small silver item is an iron item that you can carry two or more of). Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 52 (Item#). It takes 5 Action Points and uses 7 Spell Points.
