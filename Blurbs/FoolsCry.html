Ego is of vital importance to a Ruk. The illusion one casts when going into battle often sets the tone for the battle's outcome. Ergo the importance of a truly awe-inspiring Battle Cry. Conversely, a truly pitiful and embarrassing Battle Cry may be all it takes to cause your enemy to falter - as long as he/she/it is the one making a spectacle of themself. If this appeals to you, then FOOL'S CRY is just the battle spell for you. You can choose this as your offensive weapon by issuing the CAST SPELL order: C 851. From there on out, enjoy the mockery you can make of a foolish Ruk in battle. It costs 21 spell points to cast this spell.
