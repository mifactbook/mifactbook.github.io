Minding your own business, walking through hilly terrain, you are suddenly alerted to a rumbling sound that gets louder and louder. Then 'KAWHAM!' you are knocked off your feet by a falling rock. But that is no rock, it's a Rollypod. A curious little beast that has the ability to ball itself up and use its form as a weapon, making it a challenging beast to kill.
