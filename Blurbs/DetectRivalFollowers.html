Here's a spell that can save your life, especially if you're being followed by an enemy. By casting this, you'll detect Followers of other gods that are up to 2 squares away (covers a 5x5 area of squares). Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 42. It takes 12 Action Points and uses 8 Spell Points.

Note: This spell is not reliable at detecting Monsters inside Far Structures, caves, forts, etc.
