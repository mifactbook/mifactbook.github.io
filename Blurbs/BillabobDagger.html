The Billabob is an extremely effective hunter that uses its finely serated beak to skewer its pret. You can use the same approach to skewering your foes. Just take one Billabob Bill and secure it to a well-shaped stick using one Toad Leather and your Knife. The resultant double-bladed dagger has the double-penetrating action you need to take down your foe. The Billabob Dagger does Class Three Damage and you can carry two. It will take 40 Action Points to make one.
