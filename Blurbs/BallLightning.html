This Spell is a variation on the standard Lightning Spell, It is believed to have been developed in response to the Reflect spell. Instead of a single bolt, this Spell creates from 3 to 5 balls of energy that attack the target from multiple directions. This ensures that a part of Spell gets past your opponents defenses. It also reduces the potential damage that a reflected bolt would cause to its caster. To use this Spell in battle, issue the Cast Spell order: C 910. It uses 25 Spell Points each time it is cast.
