Have you ever seen eyes floating along the surface off a large pond? Them's Bortolatomus eyes. Like an iceberg, what's beneath the surface is far, far bigger than what's not. Not only do they pose a problem to anyone who enters the water but they have been seen elevated on four fin-like claws, chasing a Monster onto land. Stopping one of these deadly Creatures often takes a Missile weapon to the eye or a miracle. Sometimes both! The only sound they make is the rush of escaping air as they surface to chew you up.
