This is definitely one of the more unsightly Creatures of the island. It looks like a diseased jaguar. It hunts like a leopard in swampy terrain, utilizing its webbed paws to swim over the watery areas it can't leap over. Tangling with one is not the smartest thing unless you're a healthy, burly adventurer who carries a big knife.
