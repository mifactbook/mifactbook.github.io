--- layout: items item_name: Curved Machete item_id: 93 ---

This is a nice tool to have when traveling in the jungle. Just hack and slash with a Curved Machete to ease your way through the rough terrain (equipping not necessary). It saves wear and tear on your Haversuit. The Curved Machete is fashioned by the Far Post Jossmen and is usually acquired as a small gift from them. It is an Edged Weapon of Damage Class 1. You can carry 2.
