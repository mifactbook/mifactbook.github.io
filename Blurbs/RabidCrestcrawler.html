It's said this Swamp Creature is born foaming at the mouth and the condition only worsens with age. The resulting slobbering noise makes it fairly easy to spot. The weasle-like body is encrusted with open sores oozing a sticky lime-green pus. A Pointed weapon seems to work best - but be sure to wipe it off afterwards.
