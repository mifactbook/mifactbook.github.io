Possibly the most bizarre of the Lesser Mutates, this beastie is a cross between a Grizzly Hedgehog and a Six-headed Adder. Each of its tiny, furry little heads is lined with rows of razor-sharp fangs, each one ready to take a bite out of you. Even chopping off a head or two barely slows it down. But be persistent, because if you let down your guard, eventually, one of the heads will manage to bite you but good.
