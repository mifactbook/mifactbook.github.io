--- layout: items item_name: PigIron Shield item_id: 195 ---

This is one massive Shield. Made out of more ingots of PigIron than you could ever expect to see in one place, this bad boy requires an enormous muscle of 500 just to lug around. Forunately, you get Armor Class 9 Protection from it, making it worth its weight in, well, PigIron. Because of its size, you can only carry one.
