This may be one of the most unusual blades you have ever seen. It has a strangely curved blade that grows wider near the hilt. This sword is so light that it's like using a child's toy. However, it has good balance and it comes to an incredibly sharp point that seems ideal for attacking from the back of your mount. Too bad your stupid Plodder is alergic to combat The Cavalry Sabre is a Damage Class 2 Edged weapon. You can carry one.
