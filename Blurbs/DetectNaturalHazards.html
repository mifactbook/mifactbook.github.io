This spell allows you to detect the presence of Hidey-Holes, Deep Pits, Ponds, Tar Pits, Waterholes, Quicksand, Geysers and some others that are within two squares of you in all directions (covers a 5x5 area of squares) Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 44. It takes 16 Action Points and uses 8 Spell Points.
