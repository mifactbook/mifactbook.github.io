--- layout: creatures title: Vine Snake mob_id: 344 ---

Monster Island is wild about snakes. The Vine Snake is just another in a long-line of fanged and venomous reptiles. This one bears an uncanny resemblance to a large woody vine. Many are 20 to 30 hands long and only a hand around. Their long skinny shape lets them mimic vines and wait patiently for prey to walk by. If you are unlucky, you may be that prey.
