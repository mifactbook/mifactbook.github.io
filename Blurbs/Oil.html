You get copra oil by squeezing the oil out of dried coconut husks. This oil is useful in many Voodoo concoctions. You can carry 6 Oil. If you have less than six and have Knowledge about Voodoo and Voodoo Cauldrons, you'll collect more, automatically, and at no cost in Action Points, whenever you come upon a Coco Palm Tree.
