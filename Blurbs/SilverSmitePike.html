The Silver Smite Pike is a beautiful mixture of creature part and the skills of the Jossman ForgeMaster. The base of this fine weapon was long, yet sturdy horn from a mature Smite. The ForgeMaster has placed a thin, yet strong and very artistic coat of pure silver over the business end of the Pike. This coating has made the weapon more durable. As an added bonus, it will now do additional damage to those creatures vulnerable to Silver. The Silver Smite Pike is still a Damage Class 4 Pole Weapon. You can only carry one.
