--- layout: creatures title: Hilldog mob_id: 192 ---

This dog thinks it owns the hills. It barks incessantly at times - you'll sleep very poorly if you camp in a square where Hilldogs abound. The Hilldog has a nasty bite but otherwise isn't too tough. It is easy to spot, being white with a few blackish-gray splotches. It's almost as big and cute as a St Bernard.
