Tired of those weak little Fart Bombs? Ready for something that does a little bit more damage with a little greater style? You might try a stink bomb. To make one is easy, just issue the M 462 order and you will stew up one Stink Bomb with two oil in your voodoo cauldron. Y ou will carefully add the resulting mixture to a Fart Bomb. It will take you 20 Action Points per bomb and you can carry up to six. It is a Class 4 Missile Weapon but the Stink Bomb will help you do a little extra damage and maybe even leave a lasting impression.
