Inform Zoingot Order

A mini-quest, if you will. To receive a small reward, you must travel to a nearby Train Station and tell the local Station Master of the perils of his assistant at the hands of the Bridge Trolls. If you manage to find the Train Station, issue the INFORM ZOINGOT Order (IZ) and you will be promptly rewarded. Issuing the order will cost you 60 Action Points used in tracking down the Station Master.
