The Bloated Onion plant is commonly found in Sand & Palms terrain. Whenever you come upon this plant you'll automatically harvest the pale yellow onions. You can consume this onion by issuing the USE/CONSUME order: U 39. This onion is very potent! Eating one will enable you to NASTY FART immediately. Eating a second one before farting could be very hazardous to your health. A rarer use of this onion is known to certain Voodoo Masters. You can carry 8.
