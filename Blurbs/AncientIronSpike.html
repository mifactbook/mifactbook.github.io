--- layout: items item_name: Ancient Iron Spike item_id: 377 ---

This ancient relic, a shiny, heavy-looking iron spike was once used to "post" a Gargoyle (i.e., suspend one dead on a post by driving the iron spike through its neck - pretty gruesome). You'll do this automatically if the opportunity should ever present itself. It's the only way to keep a Gargoyle dead. But he careful not to run into a Follower of Grraggolos, the Gargoyle demigod, his followers will do their utmost to prevent your continued existence. This item may be deposited into any god's Temple Treasure Room. You can carry 1.
