--- layout: items item_name: King's Spiked Gloves item_id: 340 ---

Many, many years ago, Kro Tar, the Ninth Lavodorian King, had his Magesmiths make a wonderful pair of Gauntlets for his Royal Guard (which is believed to have numbered 200). Three small iron spikes stick out between the knuckes and the wrist, giving the gloves a dangerous look. Made from a blending of iron, gold and Kashart, the Gauntlets may be the best ever made. They provide Armour Class 4 protection. In addition, sometimes your spikes will cause additional damage to a foe, especially when wrestling. You can carry one pair.
