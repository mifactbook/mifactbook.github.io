Here's what an Ogre looks like if it was dressed up like a Mummy. It comes with the traditional off-white bandages wrapped around its body, yet these bandages are dripping with red blood. The moist bandages gives its body superior flexibility - making it move faster. This leads to the big problem if you end up in battle with one: you have to Wrestle it - and it currently holds the title of Champion Wrestler of Monster Island.
