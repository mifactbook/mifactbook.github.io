Kabuki Disciple/Priest

This offensive battle spell is cast by a Kabuki Disciple or Priest against Monster opponents in hopes of temporarily blinding him. If it overcomes his magical resistance, he'll be at a severe disadvantage in the first round of melee fighting (and that includeds him being unable to flee before the first round). Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 903. It uses up 12 Spell Points each time it is cast.
