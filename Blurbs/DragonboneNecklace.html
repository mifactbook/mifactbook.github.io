This rare, magical necklace is made from 30 or so odd pieces of Dragonbone. It's strung together with willonot and each bone fragrnent is knotted on bother sides securely. Dragonbone is the petrified remains of an ancient dragon. Most of the demigods acknowledge this necklace as a talisman of theirs. Lla Lla Na, the Wyrm demigoddess, values it as a great treasure. This bone-gray necklace also provides some magical protection, particularly against other Dragonbone items. You can carry 4.
