An interesting Voodoo Elixir was first developed by Omar Kabobdebob, legendary Voodoo Master. He was annoyed with the constant interference of Thermadons whilst travelling in Sticks. His solution was to take a Stench Gland from a Malador, 2 Purple Lotus leaves, 3 Oil, and mix them in a Cauldron. Just issue the MAKE order: M 85, and this takes 12 Action Points. The resulting elixir should be uncorked when venturing into an area overun with Thermadons. Issue the USE order: U 85, and you'll spread it around your arm pits and thighs (the two sweatiest areas). This will keep Thermadons away from you, permanently. You can carry 6 batches.
