Koala Teeth are razor sharp and extracted from the mouth of a Killer Koala Bear. If you carefully mount them on a flattened piece of bamboo an Edged weapon can be created. To craft a Tooth Sabre you need 20 Koala Teeth and must be located in Sticks terrain. Issue the MAKE order: M 55 and expend 30 Action Points. The Tooth Sabre is an Edged weapon of Damage Class 2. You can carry only one.
