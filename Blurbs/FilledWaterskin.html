This is a waterskin filled with water. Whenever you're at a location having a good water source (such as a Far Inn or clear reflecting Waterhole) you'll fill up to two skins - so that you're carrying two filled ones. If during Make Camp you are very thirsty, you'll automatically drink a filled waterskin. You can carry 2.
