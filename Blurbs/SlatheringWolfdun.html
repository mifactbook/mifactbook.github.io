--- layout: creatures title: Slathering Wolfdun mob_id: 314 ---

This rabid denizen of Shroud is a massive wolf with oversized fangs, huge clawed paws, and a massive black fur coat that hides its true size and shape as it stalks you from the shadows. A silver weapon is almost a necessity to kill this beast. Just be careful of its bite as its huge teeth and aweful jaws can put a significant hurting on even an experienced Ruk.
