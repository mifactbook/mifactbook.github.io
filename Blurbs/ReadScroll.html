By casting this Spell on a Magic Scroll in your posession, it alters the Scroll in such a way that you can now read its contents. To cast this spell issue the CAST SPELL order: C 25 (Item# of Scroll). This takes 5 Action Points and uses up 18 Spell Points. Reading a scroll destroys it but gains you the permanent knowledge of the scroll. This is why it's quite smart to first cast an Identify Scroll spell on an Unreadable Scroll.
