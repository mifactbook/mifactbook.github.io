--- layout: creatures title: Giant Slizzard mob_id: 123 ---

When skulking through a swamp, watch where you step. Too often a Monster will step on a log to cross a patch of wetlands and find himself standing on the back of a Giant Slizzard. It does not take well to being trod upon and has the perfect solution - eat the offender! In fact, eating is one of its favourite pastimes. Its large jaws can accommodate even the largest of Monsters. Its teeth can chew you up in just a few bites. Stabbing it with a Pole weapon seems to mae the most sense, as it lets you keep a bit of distance from its dripping teeth.
