Weapon Notes

  • Each weapon is classified into one of several categories. These include Missile, Pointed, Bashing, Edged, Pole, and Whip.
    • Weapons in these categories can be equipped as Primary Weapons.
  • Some Creatures are most vulnerable to attacks by specific weapons, categories of weapons or magic.
    • The first time you encounter a Creature you'll fight it with your Primary Weapon.
    • On subsequent battles you'll automatically switch weapons to one that can do more damage, if you have such a weapon and if the Creature's blurb says it will help.
  • Weapons can break or shatter against foes with very tough skins or armor.
    • During Wrestling, a Wrestle Weapon has a higher chance of breaking, the lesser weapons in particular.
