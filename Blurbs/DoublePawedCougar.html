--- layout: creatures title: Double-Pawed Cougar mob_id: 173 ---

Four legs, eight paws. Built into an incredibly muscular feline body. Wow! One encounter is more than enough for anyone. His or her mate is often nearby - ready to finish you off. If you insist on trying to kill one, an Edged weapon is best employed.The Double-Pawed Cougar considers hilly and bamboo-laden terrains its turf. Trespassers will be clawed. And clawed. And clawed. 'Til they're fresh meat for this hungry cat.
