--- layout: creatures title: Chomper mob_id: 214 ---

The Chomper is a close relative of the Muncher Beast. A digester of all things organic, it is really just a massive mastication machine. It stands a mere 10 hands tall, its body looking like a yellowish bipedal toad, but its mouth, which measures 6 x 8 hands, is more than enough to classify it as deadly. Its powerful jaws can chew up anything but rock, including monsters. Though not a naturally hostile creature, its voracious appetite makes it dangerous to be around.
