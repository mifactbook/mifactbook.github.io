Slash and decapitate the Dark with this wonderful magic Great Sword. Hack and thrust home! It's a Damage Class 5 Edged weapon, but when wielded against Monsters and creatures of the Dark, its damage is doubled. To equip it you need muscle of 140 and must be a follower of the Light. You can carry 1.
