Want to know of there's someone friendly (in theory) nearby? Cast this spell and you'll detect fellow followers of you god who are up to 2 squares away (covers a 5x5 area of squares). Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 41. It takes 8 Action Points and uses 4 Spell Points.

Note: This spell is not reliable at detecting Monster inside Far Structures, caves, forts, etc.
