The Military Pick is similar to the Mining Pick, with a few redesignes to make it more useful against living targets. The backside of the solid iron head has been sharpened to a second point. The smooth Bori Wood handle has been lengthened by adding a solid iron ball to balance the weight and provide an additional damage dealing capability. Although it could be used to break rock, it seems a terrible way to mistreat such a fine weapon. The Military Pick is massive and requires a muscle of 275 to Equip. It is a Damage Class 6 Pole weapon. You can carry one.
