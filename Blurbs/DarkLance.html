Crafted from the blackest Tarawa Wood, the Dark Lance is a formidable Weapon. It measures about 24 hands long from grip to point. Like traditional lances the user must be astride a mount for the weapon to function effectively. If you do not have a mount, you may not EQUIP with the Dark Lance. This weapon is only usable by Followers of the Dark. It is a Damage Class 9 Pole Weapon. You can possess just one. ***not in use***
