--- layout: creatures title: Bobbing Axebeak mob_id: 333 item: - name: "Axebeak Beak (#233)" url: "../Blurbs/AxeBeakBeak.html" ---

This nasty wingless bird is a true freak of nature. Its beak is deformed into a large, axe-like beak that it uses effectively to chop down onto its prey, often splitting it in half. It follows up by using its gripping talons to pluck choice pieces of flesh and meat from the carcass.
