Black Pack Rowts are the leaders of the pack! The smaller, Grey Pack Rowts follow them faithfully. Only the Black Pack Rowt is large enough to attack a Monster. If the Black Pack Rowt is able to kill its prey (you?), the entire pack will drag it back to their hole and feast. Killing a Black Pack Rowt makes a meal. You will usually spot them in sticks and hilly terrain. Being equipped with some sort of missile weapon would be awfully smart.
