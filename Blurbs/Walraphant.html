This gigantic, bloated, pink, stubby-legged beast is a bizzarity that could only happen on Monster Island. Its head ends in a huge prehensile trunk that is long enough and strong enough to wrap around your neck, pick you up and use you to pound hills flat. Add a pair of massive ivory tusks and this creature is a handful for most any Ruk.
