Another of the nasty Mutates, the Eight-jawed Yelound is some sort of twisted Hilldog product that comes equipped with no less than 8 mouths on a single head! Each mouth is more than happy to take a bite out of you. Worse yet, if it ever gets to yelping, its eight mouths can make a terrific racket. Best to slice off its head before it begins as the noise is likely to attract the rest of its pack. Then you've got REAL trouble.
