Here's a weapon that will make Light Followers flee in fear! Equippable only by muscular, successful Dark Followers (I.e. minimum 210 Muscle, rank of Disciple), this light-weight, death-seeking great sword does double damage against Monsters and Creatures of the Light. It's a Damage Class 5 Edged weapon. You can carry 1.
