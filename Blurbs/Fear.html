This offensive battle spell attempts to project a strong sense of fear into your opponent's mind. Its sole purpose is to encourage your opponent to flee. You'll only cast it against the stronger of foes. Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 928. It uses 7 Spell Points each time it's cast.
