When you hear "Ca, Ca, Ca-runch" you know that this large, dark, blue-gray colored, flying bat-like creature is approaching. The Dactyl certainly isn't that easy to kill. Perhaps the eyes would be the right spot to aim for, probably with a missile weapon of some sort. The Dactyl is often found in Jungle terrain and you better hope it doesn't spot you in the open.
