If you've ever had a tussel with the more formidable Undead, you'll know the power of silver weaponry. Trouble is, good silver is hard to find. This spell fixes that by creating a thin layer of magical silver over your current weapon making it much more effective. It is cast as an offensive spell and will cost you a whopping 12 Spell Points when you use it.
