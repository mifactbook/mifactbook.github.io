--- layout: items item_name: Mud Pie item_id: 145 ---

Wap! There's nothing like seeing the face of your opponent immediately after a well hurled Mud pie has connected. Only intelligent Mudwalkers during an Ancestral Ceremony can acquire this ability to Make and hurl these muddy missiles. Other races cannot even equip with a Mudpie. Anytime you're travelling in Low Hills terrain and you've less than 9 Mudpies, you'll Make more and at no cost in Action Points. You can carry 9.
