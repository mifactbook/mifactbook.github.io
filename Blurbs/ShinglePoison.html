The thought of coating a weapon of yours with a nasty, poisonous substance has been in the back of your mind for quite some time. You can now create Shingle Poison by combining 2 Acorn Hearts and 2 Oil in a Voodoo Cauldron. To do this, issue the MAKE order: M 62 and expend 16 Action Points. This produces 2 batches of Shingle Poison. You can carry 4 batches. To use, issue the EQUIP order: E 62, and you will put a batch of it on your already equipped Edged, Pointed, or Pole weapon just before hand-to-hand battle next occurs. This is extremely fast-acting poison that causes more damage to be inflicted - the poison does not linger in the victim.
