Yes, these are those slow-moving guys with outstretched arms, wrapped in gray moldy bandages. They resemble the ones you last saw in an Abbot & Costello Meet the Mummy movie. They're the guardians of Graveyards and Tombs. Battling them is a difficult task, as Bashing weapons don't seem very effective. When their decaying flesh is exposed to the outside air, they scream with pain which makes you wonder how alive the undead actually are.
