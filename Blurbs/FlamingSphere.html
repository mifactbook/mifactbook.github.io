Mage Spell

This offensive spell is similar in many ways to the more common Fireball spell. The difference is that this spell creates a much larger ball of flame that rolls across the ground. The Flaming Sphere rolls across your opponent burning him severely and continues on to incinerate anything that might be behind him. Campsites and other trusty possessions have been destroyed by the continued actions of this Spell. To use this Spell in battle, issue the Cast Spell order: C 909. It uses 18 Spell Points each time it is cast.
