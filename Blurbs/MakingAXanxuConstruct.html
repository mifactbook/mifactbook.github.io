Making a Xanxu Construct

Making a construct is not a small task. It requires more items than a single Ruk can carry and is extremely complicated. To build one requires at least six members of your group to gather in a square and create a Cache. In the Cache you must place 6 Xanxu Snake Heads, 8 Xanxu Leg Joints, 8 Xanxu Webbed Feet, 2 Xanxu Bear Carcasses, 4 Black Lotus Leaves, 2 White Lotus Leaves, and 4 Somanda Dust. Then with all six group members in the square one monster must issue the MAKE CONSTRUCT Order (M 466). This monster will gather the group members together and everyone will take turns stewing the pot and smearing the resulting paste all over the parts. When it is done, the construct will be fully functionalthough very odd looking.

Using the Silly Thing

As usual, using things on Monster Island is not as easy as it sounds. The Xanxu Construct is a strange beast and requires considerable effort to use it. Therefore, when you use the Construct (U 466 [# APs]), you will only get about a quarter of the work out of it that you put into it. Bummer that.
