--- layout: creatures title: Pummel Bear mob_id: 168 loc: - name: "Large Cave" url: "../Locations/LargeCave.html" ---

A common denizen of Large Caves, this bear likes to sleep a lot. Sure, it has a great growl, but what's really impressive is its mouth. The large, furry-brown jaws can open to cavernous size, displaying pointy teeth. Rows of 'em! This bear can absorb lots of damage and create a lot as well. Killing one nets you a mountain of tasty bear meat.
