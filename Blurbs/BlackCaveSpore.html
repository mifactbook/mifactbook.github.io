When exploring one of the small caves, it is somewhat likely that you'll find a part of the cave's wall covered with Black Cave Spore. If you've a Tooth Sabre, you'll automatically try to pry off a couple of the hard-to-find spores of this fungi. You can carry up to three clumps of spores.
