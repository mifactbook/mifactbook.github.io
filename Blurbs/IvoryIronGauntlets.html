--- layout: items item_name: Ivory Iron Gauntlets item_id: 418 ---

Just what every "Good Guy" needs, a pair of Ivory Iron Gauntlets. Providing an excellent Armor Class 3 protection to your good-guy hands, they also cut a dashing figure when you are wandering around town. Best of all, they seem to magically repel dirt and grime and are often the only clean thing on a Ruk. You can carry one pair.
