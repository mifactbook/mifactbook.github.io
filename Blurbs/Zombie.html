Yes, the Undead walk on Monster Island! Few know the secret to their existence but most believe that skilled Voodoo practitioners have power over them. A Zombie is very difficult to eradicate. It never uses any sophisticated weaponry but rather relies on its teeth and superior strength to rip you from limb to limb. An Edged weapon might be effective against it.
