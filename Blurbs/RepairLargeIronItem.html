Rather than paying a Forger to repair a large iron item, you can magically repair it yourself by casting this spell. (A large iron item is an iron item that you can only carry one) Issue the CAST SPELL order: C 53 (Item#). It takes 8 Action Points and uses 12 Spell Points.
