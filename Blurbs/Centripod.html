--- layout: creatures title: Centripod mob_id: 309 ---

Imagine a whirling disc with 100 multi-jointed legs. Now imagine each leg is tipped with cutting claws. Still further, imagine the central disc having 100 mouths each waiting for a piece of food. Well, now you are imagining the Centripod. It attacks by rotating rapidly with each of its legs alternately trying to take a piece out of you. Not a pleasant creature to meet in a tight spot.
