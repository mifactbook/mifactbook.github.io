Each Ocular is a perfectly shaped Rosyte stone, approximately 1/6 of a hand in diameter. You can carry a maximum of 2000 of these coins. Scribed (or stamped) on the obverse side is the picture of some king (very hard to make out). The reverse side bears these words: "One Ocular" and "Lavodorian Treasury - The Civilized Kingdom".
