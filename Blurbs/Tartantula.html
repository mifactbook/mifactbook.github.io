--- layout: creatures title: Tartantula mob_id: 321 item: - name: "Tar Sac (#229)" url: "../Blurbs/TarSac.html" ---

This hideous representative of spiderdom is easily the biggest, nastiest, smelliest spider to walk the Island. It has a massive, bloated, hairy body with eight enormous, multi-jointed legs. Its head has almost 100 eyes that can stare down a herd of Plodders and its crushing mandibles can crush stone to dust. In addition, it has a nasty habit of spitting hot tar at its prey, burning and poisoning them to death.
