A devilishly clever little potion, Elixir of Poison makes use of some of the arcane Black Voodoo and equally arcane Zoingot Wizardry to make a nasty little missile weapon. To make one combine one Black Cave Spore with one Guano Plasm and cook them along with one Oil for 18 Action Points in your Voodoo Cauldron. Equip it like any other missile weapon. Just be sure to step back when your opponent starts to spew! You can carry six.
