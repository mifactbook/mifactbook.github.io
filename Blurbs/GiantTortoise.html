These large creatures are found along the shore or in larger pools of water inland. They are only dangerous if you attack them or if they discover you in a Hidey-Hole. They are nearly impossible to kill through their hardened shell. The only vulnerable spot on their body is a tiny soft area in the centre of their forehead. A spear is very useful in attacking the tortoise from a distance while you probe for this spot.
