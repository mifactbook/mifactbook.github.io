--- layout: items item_name: Zoingo Ruby Dagger item_id: 371 ---

Crafted by one of the skilled Zoingot jewelers, this ornamental dagger looks precious. It's just over two hands in length and its nicest features are the four small rubies that decorate the hilt. Some Jossmen prize such objects. This dagger lacks a sharp edge and would break easily if it were used as a weapon. You can carry 4.
