--- layout: items item_name: Broad Sword item_id: 101 ---

This is a heavy wide-bladed sword that was obviously designed for war. Its dead weight and wide shape were not designed for elegant swordplay. This is a weapon strong warriors used to beat down their enemies defenses. The added weight of the Broadsword requires a Muscle of 125 to equip. It is a Damage Class 4 Edged Weapon. You can carry one.
