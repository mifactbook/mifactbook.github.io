--- layout: items item_name: Dragon Flagon item_id: 385 ---

This dark black, dragon-shaped, heavy flagon is made from some unknown and virtually indestructable metal. It may actually be a portrayal of Kadar Black the legendary Dragon demigod. It is one of the ancient relic vessels. Long ago, certain liquids were drunk from it that gave special powers to the holder - powers associated with Dragons. Nowadays, discovering such liquids seems very slim. However, it would also make a splendid addition to any god's Temple Treasure Room. You can carry 3.
