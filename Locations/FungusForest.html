You dig for the Blackened 'Shrooms as the Jossmen requested. Your hole reaches 9 hands deeps and suddenly the ground seems to disappear below you. You instinctively grasp a nearby root and hang suspended over the strangest jungle you have ever seen.You quickly pull yourself back up and examine the forest from a safer angle. You note that the cavern is quite large. Clumps of many different types of fungus grow below. You see occasional clearings in the fungus below. As you watch carefully, you glimpse movement in one of the clearings. Unfortunately you can't see what it was. The good news is that some of the growths glow and provide light. You are convinced you can reach the forest by attaching your Jute Rope to a sturdy Rock Mushroom.
