Far Exotica

One of the more resplendent of Jossman buildings is the Far Exotica. Made from top quality bamboo and stone with a thatched roof, its owner has obviously ploughed some of his profits into its reconstruction. It is a cross between department store and a bureau de change.

Certain Towns are known to specialise in making certain exotic consumer goods, and so the wise buyer can make a profit transporting them to where they're in demand.

The Exotica's other main line is currency exchange. Should you be reaching your carrying limit for Oculars, they will exchange them for Golden Ocular coins. There is no charge for this and only a small charge to change Oculars with any of the other more exotic coins. But, before you ask, they don't deal in Bit Coins!
