Ancient Graveyard

Order
Action Points
Items
LV
16
Digging

Dozens of tombstones litter the environs of this graveyard. The tombstones come in all shapes and sizes. All are extremely weathered, almost battered looking. Several holes exist where, no doubt, someone long ago exhumed a corpse in hopes of the loot. The place has a very eerie feel to it, almost as if you were being watched by something or some things. Craggy trees and high bushes surround the site, almost as if the tombstones were being protected from direct sunlight. To Loot 'n Vandalize here is definitely going to require some sort of digging implement.
