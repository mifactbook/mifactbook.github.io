The large mouth and roomy interior of a Great Cave allows very large Creatures to live inside. Still, it's too dangerous to ride a Mount inside - the floor of the cave is choppy throughout. Those fully armoured and heavily armed, with excellent weapon skills, may wish to venture inside. You need extensive lighting as you'll be in there a long time (160 Action Points). Enter only if you're very healthy and prepared for anything.
