Large Cave

Order
Action Points
Items
U 33
60
3 Light

Amid a pile of rocks you come upon a large depression in the ground. Upon closer examination it appears to be the mouth of a Large Cave. You can easily fit through the entrance, if you have the courage. To explore the cave you will need 3 Torches. Write the USE order: U 33 when you are located here and you will enter the cave for 60 Action Points and explore it thoroughly. Keep in mind, the larger the cave, the larger the Creature who may dwell inside.
