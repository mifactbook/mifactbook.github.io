Often hidden in Jungle terrain, this large, half-sunken structure is composed of whethered, but sturdy stone. The front is about 40 hands wide and the sides go back 75 or so hands. Stone steps descend six hands to a large stone door. The door is quite solid and features a large key hole (you can't see anything through it). Curiously, the steps lack any sort of sand, dirt or debris on them. At one point you think you hear a distant thud or dull ring, but you can't be sure if it's not just your mind playing tricks on you. You tried budging the stone door - it doesn't even give you a hint that it's going to move. Should you be sealed with a large lockpick, you may try to enter by issuing the U 33 order. Sufficient light for 80 Action Points of exploration is required
