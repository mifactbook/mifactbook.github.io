Kabuki Prayer Stone

This is a large, white stone, roughly 20 hands high, 8 wide, and 8 deep. At eye level is a prism embedded in the stone. You stare at the stone for quite some time. Suddenly the sun comes out from under a cloud and shines directly into the prism. Expecting to see a rainbow of sorts, you are suprised to see writing. It says "The Great Kabuki, God of Light, welcomes your prayers and loyalty. Kowtow to me Here." To do this, issue the KOWTOW order: K (Action Points).
