Survey Logpost

A thick pole, 12-14 hands high, is situated out in the most open spot of this square. Stenciled onto ot is an "L.K." and beneath that is a number. You try moving the logpost; it doesn't budge. You try running a dozen steps and then clanging your body against it - you succeed in vibrating it a bit. Very solid. You're sort of impressed.
