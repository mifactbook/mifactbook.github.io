Koma Den

Before you is a strange arrangement of bamboo poles and shoots, rocks and logs, and other natural building materials. As you circle and study it, you note several varieties of snakes slithering around and through it. The structure, if you could call it one, protrudes 3 or 4 hands above the ground and who knows how many below. You think about moving closer, but those snakes don't look friendly and some are probably poisonous.
