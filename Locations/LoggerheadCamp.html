Loggerhead Camp

Order
Action Points
Items
LV
16
 

Usually found in Sticks or Low Hills terrain, this is a crude settlement by anyone's standards. Set against the side of a hill are a haphazard assortment of bamboo poles and tree branches that form a series of ugly-looking huts. Standing down-wind from such a place is quite unpleasant. If that's not reason enough to stay clear of such squares, a typical encampment has a dozen to two dozen jabbering Loggerheads, all well-armed.
