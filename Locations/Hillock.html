Toward the center of the square is this mysterious mound, a Hillock. It stands out because it doesn't look completely natural and you can't figure out why. A strangeness seems to emanate from it. Circling around it several times you spot a hidden opening near the top. If you're feeling brave, return to the square with a Torch and issue the USE order: U 33. It will take 18 Action Points to investigate fully. Oh, putting your nose to the ground, you note a strange smell, er, stench.
