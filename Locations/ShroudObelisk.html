Shroud Obelisk

On the highest point in the square sits a finely polished granite obelisk. It stands over 40 hands tall and the top appears rounded at the corners. You look the huge stone over carefully. Eventually you find writing that says, "Kneel at this spot in awe of the awesome Dread of SHROUD, the Dark God." If you wish to do so, issue the KOWTOW order: K (Action Points).
