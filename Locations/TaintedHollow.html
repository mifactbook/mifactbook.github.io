This foreboding, small valley is densely filled with assorted palm trees. It's so dark and the trees are so thick that you'll need plenty of lighting, a machete, something to help you find your way and a long 120 Action Points to explore it properly. A Mount cannot penetrate the Hollow - it's too tangled in there for a mount to find its way through. Of peculiar note is that the trees seem too alive, if such a thing were possible. Without appropriate special protection, it would be anywhere from very foolhardy to extremely dangerous to enter the confines of a Tainted Hollow.
